Frequently Asked Questions



How much does tree removal cost?

It depends on the size, location, and condition of the tree. We provide free estimates so you’ll always know the cost upfront.



Can I keep the firewood or woodchips?

Yes, however we do also offer cleanup and hauling with our tree removal services.



Do I need to be home for the service?

It's not a requirement, but be sure to clarify before the work begins.



When is the best time to schedule tree services?

We provide tree care year-round, so you can schedule service whenever you need it. That said, certain times of year are better for specific jobs:

  • Late winter to early spring: best for pruning and trimming, since trees are dormant and easier to shape.
  • Fall: a good time to prepare your property before winter storms.
  • After storms: emergency removals are available any time of year if a tree becomes hazardous.

No matter the season, we’re here to help keep your trees safe and your property looking its best.
